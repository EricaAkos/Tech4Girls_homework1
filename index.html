My Journey into Tech

From Curiosity to Coding

Meet Erica, a young woman who recently decided to embark on a new and exciting journey into the world of technology and coding. She had always been curious about how websites and applications
worked, but never imagined she would start learning how to build them herself.

The First Steps

Erica began by learning the basics of HTML, the foundation of web development. She created her first web page, adding headings, paragraphs, and event formatting her text using bold, italic, and underline
elements.

Every new tag she learned brought a sense of accomplishment.

She quickly became comfortable with HTML elements and is eager to explore more.

Next on her list: learning more HTML elements and CSS to style her web pages!

Challenges and Groth

The journey wasn't always easy. Erica encountered errors, bugs, and challenges that tested her patience. But she never gave up. She knew with every mistake, she was one step closer to becoming a
confident coder.

At the end of program, Erica can build simple web pages and will slowly venture into JavaScript to make her projects interactive.

The Road Ahead

Erica knows that the road to mastering tech is long, but she is excited for the future. She's evn started sharing her prograss on social media to inspire others, especially other women, to dive into the world
of coding.

Conclusion

Erica's journey is just begining. With passion, determination, and the ability to never stop learning, she knows that the posibilities in tech are endless.
