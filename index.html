My Experience As A Tech4Girls Scholar

Soft Skills

As a girl who didn't know much about IT, I decided to grab this opportunity as soon as a friend introduced HACSA Tech4Girls to me. At the begining of the program, we started with soft skills, namely; leadership, problem solving, critical thinking, social media marketing, public speaking, and entrepreneurship.

Technology

We later started with the technology aspect of the program which is fun but daunting as well.
Like the saying goes "nothing comes easy", but I believe with time and constant practice, it's going to be much easier and fun.

I would like to recommend this course to all other girls and women out there. Let's all come out of our comfort zone and gain new and educative experiences for better days ahead.
